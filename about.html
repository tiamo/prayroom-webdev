About

Prayroom is not about religion. This is about practicing what Jesus gave to every believer.

We firmly believe that simultaneous prayer of large groups of people is the most powerful tool for establishing the Kingdom of Heaven here on the Earth. There is no sickness and poverty, fear and hatred in Heaven.

So let’s bring Heaven to Earth by uniting in prayers over personal requests, worldwide praise and worship. God gave us the tool, now is our turn - use it. Prayroom is a non-denominational ministry brought to you by means of modern technologies.

Let’s rock this world by God’s Love!
